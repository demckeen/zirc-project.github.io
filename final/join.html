Joining the Birmingham Chamber of Commerce

There are certainly things that the Birmingham Chamber of Commerce can provide to the public, but the best way to ensure you are receiving all the benefits of what the Chamber can offer is to become a member. Membership levels start at $100(Nonprofit) and range up to $1000 for the most benefits offered.

Nonprofit Level - $100

Nonprofit Organizations that are registered either at the state or federal level as such are able to join for an annual contribution of $100. Organizations at this level of membership receive the following benefits:

  • Listing in our Directory
  • Use of the Chamber logo on your website and materials
  • Access to members only events
  • Access to contribute on membership issues
  • A voice in local Government through the Chamber
  • Discounted pricing for Chamber events
  • Access to our professional development and educational events
  • Free event listings in our City Events Calendar

Bronze Level - $200

Businesses and other organizations may join chamber membership at the bronze level with an annual contribution of $200. Members at this level of membership will receive all the same benefits as the nonprofit level as well as:

  • Discount of 20% on site advertising
  • Two conference room rentals
  • One free advertisement in our chamber newsletter per year
  • One free member feature in our chamber newsletter per year

Silver Level - $400

Those who wish to join at the Silver Level will make a contribution of $1000 annually. Members at this level of membership will receive all the same benefits as the lower levels as well as the following:

  • Unlimited conference room rentals
  • One free 3 month member feature ad on our website
  • Discount of 30% on all other advertising
  • Free tickets to two exclusive chamber events per year
  • One free social media feature on all our channels

Gold Level - $1000

This is our top level of membership. Premier members will achieve this level of membership with an annual donation of $1000. Members at this level will receive all the same benefits as the lower levels as well as the following:

  • An exlusive meeting with the board to discuss strategy for the year
  • Logo displayed at all Chamber Events
  • Social Media feature on all our channels once per quarter
  • Free tickets to all exclusive chamber events during the year
  • Featured advertisement in all chamber newsletters
  • Advance access to special events throughout the year
