Article Headline

Welcome to the weathermoment.com site planning page. This website will be devoted to those who take a brief moment each day to check in on the weather and their small town. It will have basic weather information and relevant town information for each of the towns. There will be a page dedicated to each town and their specific information such as events and city level community concerns, as well as a page only for looking at storm data and current concerns for severe weather events. This section will also include resources for advisable safety measures for various relevant severe weather events in the area.
