Typography

In this site we have two primary typefaces:

  • Comfortaa - Used in heading elements
  • Nanum Gothic - Used in body text

The primary heading found in the header is sized at 3em. Headings at h2 level are sized at 1.75em and h3 at 1.25em.

The main navigation bar is in Nanum Gothic and sized at 1.2rem. The main body text is at 18px, while the footer is at 16px aside from the "Last Updated" line which is sized at 12px. Both the main body and footer are also Nanum Gothic.
