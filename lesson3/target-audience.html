Target Audience

The individuals living in these three towns are overwhelmingly white, the median income is at a lower middle class level, and while Soda Springs and Preston have average median age, Fish Haven is much older and much smaller. The target audience is likely primarily 35-65, white, and middle class.

Personas

Susan

Susan is a retiree in Fish Haven who likes to work in her garden. She needs to routinely check weather conditions to see if it is suitable for her activity and healthy for her plants. She also likes to be plugged in to what is happening in her community.

She is:

  • 65 years old
  • White
  • Middle class
  • Active
  • Exclusively visiting from desktop
  • Educated with completion of some college level courses

John

John is an office manager who lives in Soda Springs. He is married with two children and is a runner. He need to routeinely check the weather so he is dressed appropriately for his running activities or when he wants to spend time outside with his children.

He is:

  • 37 years old
  • White
  • Middle class
  • Active
  • Primarily visiting from a mobile browser
  • Educated with an advanced degree

Scenarios:

  • I need to see if it will be too cold for my plants tonight
  • What should I wear on my run today?
  • Will it be raining for the kids athletic event?
  • What engaging activities are available for seniors?
  • Are there any family friendly events this weekend?
  • Do my kids need a coat today?
